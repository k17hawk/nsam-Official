NSAM
Nepal Student Association of Microbiology(NSAM) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization lead by youths. It is located at Central Campus of Technology, Dharan. It is established as a research thought hub in eastern Nepal. It was officially registered as an NGO in District adminstrative office, sunsari (3058/075/076) in 2019.
